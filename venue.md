<section class="venue-context" aria-label="About Garching and Munich">
  <p>
    The Garching Campus is located about <strong>30 minutes by subway (U-Bahn, line U6)</strong> from the heart of Munich. The city is world-renowned for its blend of <strong>Bavarian tradition</strong> and <strong>cutting-edge innovation</strong>, a place where you can enjoy a warm <strong>brezn (pretzel)</strong> and a cold beer in one of the many historic beer gardens, while also being at the doorstep of leading research and technology hubs.
  </p>
</section>
